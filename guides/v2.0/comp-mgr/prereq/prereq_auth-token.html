在管理面板输入你的认证密钥

To upgrade your Magento Commerce version or to 从Magento开源版升级到Magento企业版, you must be authorized to access the Magento Commerce repository. Contact Magento Support if you have questions.

To enter your authentication keys:

  1. Log in to the Magento管理面板 as an administrator.
  2. Click System > Tools > Web Setup Wizard.

  3. Click System Configuration.

  4. Enter your public and private authentication keys in the provided fields.

  5. Click Save Config.
